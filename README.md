## **Foundation of the DevOps Step-By-Step Roadmap and Vision**

 This repository represents the foundation of **DevOps Step-By-Step**. It brings together the roadmap, structured learning paths, course outlines, hands-on labs, and supporting materials that define the vision and provide a clear, step-by-step progression for learners and practitioners.

We also launched additional platforms to extend our mentorship and community experience. These spaces help us scale collaboration, enable broader contribution, and create more room for community projects, technical articles, and mentorship-focused engagement while keeping this repository as the central reference point for the roadmap and learning content.

Explore the extended ecosystem below.

## 🌐 The Extended Ecosystem

As the **DevOps Step-By-Step** initiative grew, we created additional spaces that help us scale mentorship, community collaboration, and publishing. These platforms work together with this repository to provide a broader experience: structured learning here, and expanded engagement through dedicated hubs.

### 1️⃣ Elmentor Program (Mentorship)
*A dedicated mentorship space designed to deliver guided learning, structured support, and focused coaching paths. It complements the learning roadmap in this repository by offering a more interactive mentorship experience for individuals and teams.*
* 🌐 **Official Website:** [elmentorprogram.com](http://elmentorprogram.com/)
* 🐙 **GitHub Organization:** [github.com/ElmentorProgram](https://github.com/ElmentorProgram)
* A dedicated space for mentorship and structured guidance that complements the roadmap in this repository.

### 2️⃣ DevOps Visions (Community & Blog)
*A community hub for open source collaboration and technical publishing. It’s where we share articles, patterns, and project work that grow out of the roadmap and training materials in this repository, and where contributors can participate in building and improving community-driven initiatives.*
* ✍️ **Technical Blog:** [devopsvisions.github.io/blog/](https://devopsvisions.github.io/blog/)
* 🐙 **GitHub Organization:** [github.com/DevOpsVisions](https://github.com/DevOpsVisions)
